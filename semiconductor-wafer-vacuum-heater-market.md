<p><ul><li>Single Zone Vacuum Heater</li><li>Multi Zone Vacuum Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wafer vacuum heater market features two primary types: single zone vacuum heaters and multi zone vacuum heaters. Single zone vacuum heaters offer uniform heating across an entire wafer surface, ideal for simpler applications. In contrast, multi zone vacuum heaters provide independent temperature control across various zones, allowing for more precise thermal management and optimization for complex processes. This flexibility makes multi zone heaters preferable for advanced semiconductor fabrication, enhancing efficiency and performance in the delicate manufacturing process.</p></p>
