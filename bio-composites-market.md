<p><p>The bio-composites market consists of several key processing types: </p><p>1. **Extrusion** involves forcing the bio-composite formulation through a die to create continuous shapes, suitable for applications like films and sheets.</p><p>2. **Compression Molding** uses heated molds to shape bio-composites by applying pressure, ideal for producing complex parts.</p><p>3. **Injection Molding** injects molten bio-composite materials into molds, allowing for high-precision components.</p><p>4. **Others** includes methods like pultrusion and thermoforming, catering to specialized applications in various industries.</p></p>
