<p><p>A Powder X-ray Diffractometer (PXRD) is an analytical tool used to characterize crystallographic structure through the diffraction of X-rays. It enables the identification and quantification of crystalline phases in a sample, making it essential in various fields such as materials science, chemistry, and pharmaceuticals. The technique involves the measurement of the angles and intensities of scattered X-rays, which helps in determining detailed information about crystallite size, strain, and phase composition.</p><p>The PXRD market is anticipated to witness significant growth, driven by advancements in technology and an increasing demand for material characterization.
