<p><ul><li>Solar Control</li><li>Convection Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car glass insulation film market is primarily divided into solar control and convection control types. Solar control films are designed to reduce heat buildup by reflecting or absorbing solar radiation, minimizing glare and enhancing comfort within the vehicle. In contrast, convection control films work by improving thermal efficiency, reducing heat loss through glass surfaces. Together, these films enhance passenger comfort, protect interiors from UV damage, and contribute to energy efficiency in vehicles, making them essential for modern automotive design.</p></p>
