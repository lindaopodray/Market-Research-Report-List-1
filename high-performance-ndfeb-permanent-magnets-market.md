<p><strong>The High-performance NdFeB Permanent Magnets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonded NdFeB Magnets</li><li>Sintered NdFeB Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-performance NdFeB permanent magnets market comprises two main types: bonded and sintered NdFeB magnets. Bonded NdFeB magnets are produced by combining powdered magnet material with a polymer binder, allowing for complex shapes and improved mechanical strength, making them suitable for various applications like electric motors and sensors. Sintered NdFeB magnets, on the other hand, are created through a powder metallurgy process, offering superior magnetic properties and higher density, making them ideal for applications requiring strong and compact magnets, such as in electric vehicles and industrial machinery.</p></p>
