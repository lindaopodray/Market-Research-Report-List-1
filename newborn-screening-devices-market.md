<p><ul><li>Dry Blood Spot Test</li><li>Hearing Screen Test</li><li>Critical Congenital Heart Diseases (CCHD) Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Newborn screening devices encompass various tests aimed at early detection of health conditions in infants. The Dry Blood Spot Test involves collecting a small blood sample to screen for metabolic and genetic disorders. The Hearing Screen Test assesses auditory capabilities to identify potential hearing impairments early. The Critical Congenital Heart Diseases (CCHD) Test uses pulse oximetry to detect heart defects that may not be apparent at birth. Each test serves a crucial role in promoting timely intervention and improving newborn health outcomes.</p></p>
