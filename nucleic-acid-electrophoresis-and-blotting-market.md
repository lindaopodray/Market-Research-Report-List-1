<p><p>Nucleic Acid Electrophoresis and Blotting are essential laboratory techniques used for the separation and analysis of nucleic acids, such as DNA and RNA. Electrophoresis involves applying an electric field to a gel matrix, causing nucleic acids to migrate based on their size and charge. Blotting techniques, such as Southern and Northern blotting, allow for the transfer of these nucleic acids from gels to membranes, followed by detection using specific probes. Together, these methods are pivotal in molecular biology, genetics, and diagnostics.</p><p>The Nucleic Acid Electrophoresis and Blotting Market is poised for significant growth, with an expected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 12.6% during the forecast period. These factors collectively contribute to the robust outlook of the market.</p></p>
