<p><p>The wooden pallet pooling market includes two main types: pallet rental and pallet pooling. Pallet rental involves leasing pallets for short-term use, allowing businesses to manage costs without long-term commitments. In contrast, pallet pooling refers to a shared system where companies collectively use and return pallets, enhancing efficiency and sustainability. This model reduces overhead costs and waste while promoting resource sharing, making it an attractive option for industries requiring consistent pallet supply throughout their supply chain operations.</p></p>
