<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery market can be divided into two main types: heat exchangers and boilers. Heat exchangers utilize the waste heat from exhaust gases to preheat incoming fluids, enhancing overall energy efficiency in various industries. Conversely, boilers harness this waste heat to produce steam or hot water for power generation or heating applications. Both technologies aim to minimize energy loss and reduce emissions, contributing to overall sustainability and cost savings in industrial processes.</p></p>
