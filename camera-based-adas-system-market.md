<p><p>Camera-based ADAS systems enhance vehicle safety and driving convenience through various camera types. Front cameras, mounted on the windshield, enable features like lane departure warnings and forward collision alerts. Rear cameras, placed at the back, assist in parking and obstacle detection. Driver monitoring systems use interior cameras to track driver alertness and prevent fatigue-related accidents. Surround view systems combine multiple cameras to provide a 360-degree view, aiding in parking and low-speed maneuvering. These systems collectively improve safety, awareness, and driving efficiency.</p></p>
