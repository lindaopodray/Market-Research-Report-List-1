<p><p>The packaged salt market consists of three primary types: Rock Salt, Solar Salt, and Evap Salt. Rock Salt is naturally mined and often used for de-icing and cooking. Solar Salt is obtained through evaporation of seawater in solar ponds, resulting in high purity and large crystals, commonly used in culinary and industrial applications. Evap Salt is generated by evaporating brine in controlled environments, producing fine, pure salt ideal for food processing. Each type serves distinct consumer needs and industries.</p></p>
