<p><ul><li>Anion Ion-exchange Resin</li><li>Cation Ion-exchange Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor grade ion exchange resins market comprises two main types: anion ion-exchange resins and cation ion-exchange resins. Anion resins are used for removing negatively charged ions from solutions, crucial for achieving ultra-pure water and preventing contamination in semiconductor manufacturing. Conversely, cation resins target positively charged ions, facilitating the removal of unwanted cations. Both types play a vital role in maintaining the high purity levels required in the semiconductor industry, ensuring the reliability and performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
