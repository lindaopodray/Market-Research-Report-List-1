<p><ul><li>Cementless</li><li>Cemented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temporary shoulder spacers market is divided into two main types: cementless and cemented spacers. Cementless spacers are designed for easier insertion and removal without the use of bone cement, promoting natural healing and reducing complications. In contrast, cemented spacers utilize bone cement to secure the implant, providing greater initial stability and support during recovery. Both types serve to temporarily replace the shoulder joint in patients undergoing revisions or infections, ensuring optimal function and mobility during the treatment process.</p></p>
