<p><p>The Aviation Container Board market is categorized into two main types: Main Deck Pallet and Lower Deck Pallet. Main Deck Pallets are utilized for cargo on the upper level of wide-body aircraft, designed to accommodate larger, heavier shipments. In contrast, Lower Deck Pallets are used for loading in the aircraft's lower baggage compartments, optimized for smaller, lighter packages. Both types play a crucial role in efficient cargo management and maximizing space utilization in air freight operations.</p></p>
