<p><p>The Hybrid and Electric Vehicle Integrated Drive Unit (IDU) market comprises three key components: Electric Motor, Power Electronics, and Gearbox. The Electric Motor converts electrical energy into mechanical power, driving the vehicle. Power Electronics manage energy flow between the battery and motor, ensuring efficiency and control. The Gearbox optimizes torque and speed for smooth performance. Together, these components form a compact, efficient system that enhances vehicle performance, reduces emissions, and supports the transition to sustainable mobility in hybrid and electric vehicles.</p></p>
