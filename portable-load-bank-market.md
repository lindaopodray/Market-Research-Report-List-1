<p><p>The portable load bank market comprises three main types: resistive load banks, reactive load banks, and resistive-reactive load banks. Resistive load banks primarily convert electrical energy into heat, ideal for testing generators and uninterruptible power supplies. Reactive load banks simulate the inductive or capacitive loads, useful for assessing power factor and generator performance. Resistive-reactive load banks combine both functionalities, providing a versatile solution for comprehensive testing. Each type caters to different testing needs in various industries, ensuring reliable power supply performance.</p></p>
