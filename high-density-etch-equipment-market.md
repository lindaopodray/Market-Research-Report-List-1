<p><ul><li>Dry Etching Equipment</li><li>Wet Etching Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high density etch equipment market is primarily categorized into dry etching and wet etching equipment. Dry etching employs plasma or reactive gases to precisely remove material from substrates, offering high resolution and control, making it ideal for semiconductor fabrication. In contrast, wet etching utilizes chemical solutions to dissolve specific materials, providing a simpler and often less expensive alternative for patterned etching. Both methods are crucial in the microfabrication processes for electronics, serving distinct applications based on precision and material compatibility.</p></p>
