<p><p>Smart Worker Market encompasses various connectivity technologies essential for enhancing operational efficiency and communication. BLE/Bluetooth provides short-range, low-energy connections ideal for wearable devices and task management. LPWAN (Low Power Wide Area Network) offers long-range communications with minimal power consumption, suitable for remote monitoring and IoT applications. WFAN (Wireless Fidelity Area Network) supports high-speed data transfer over longer distances, making it ideal for collaborative environments. Together, these technologies empower smart workers by enabling seamless connectivity, real-time data access, and improved productivity.</p></p>
