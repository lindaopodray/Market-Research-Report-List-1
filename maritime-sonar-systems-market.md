<p><p>The Maritime Sonar Systems market consists of various technologies tailored for underwater exploration. Single Beam Scanning Sonar Systems emit a single acoustic beam to measure depths and detect objects directly below the vessel. Multi-beam Sonar Systems utilize multiple beams to create detailed, high-resolution maps of the seafloor. Side Scan Sonar Systems are designed to produce images of the seafloor and subsurface structures by scanning sideways, while "Other" categories may include innovative or specialized sonar technologies catering to unique maritime applications.</p></p>
