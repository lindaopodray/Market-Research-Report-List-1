<p><p>The automotive seatbelt pre-tensioners market consists of several types, including buckle pre-tensioners, retractor pre-tensioners, and others. Buckle pre-tensioners activate upon collision to pull the seatbelt tight at the buckle, enhancing occupant restraint. Retractor pre-tensioners work similarly, tightening the seatbelt from the retractor side during a crash. Other types may include advanced systems that integrate with airbag deployments or smart technologies. Together, these components significantly improve safety performance in vehicles, reducing occupant movement during impacts.</p></p>
