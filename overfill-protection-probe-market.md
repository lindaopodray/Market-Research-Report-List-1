<p><p>The Overfill Protection Probe market includes various types: Light Sensing, Immersion Sensing, and Others. Light Sensing probes utilize optical technologies to detect fluid levels based on light interruption or reflection, providing non-contact monitoring. Immersion Sensing probes are submerged in liquids to measure levels using resistive or capacitive methods, ensuring direct interaction with the fluid. Other types encompass diverse technologies like ultrasonic or radar systems, offering additional options for effective overfill detection in various industrial applications.</p></p>
