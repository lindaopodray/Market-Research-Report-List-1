<p><strong>The Down-draught Gasifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating fluidized bed (CFB)</li><li>Bubbling fluidized bed (BFB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The down-draught gasifier market features two primary technologies: Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) and Bubbling Fluidized Bed (BFB). CFB gasifiers utilize a continuous flow of gas and particulate materials, allowing for better heat and mass transfer, making them suitable for larger capacities and diverse feedstocks. In contrast, BFB gasifiers involve solid particles suspended in a gas stream, providing a more controlled reaction environment ideal for smaller-scale applications and varied biomass input. Both technologies enhance syngas production efficiency.</p></p>
