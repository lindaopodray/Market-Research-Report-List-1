<p><p>The waste fill level sensor market primarily comprises two types: ultrasonic and laser sensors. Ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves to measure the distance to waste material, providing reliable fill level detection in various environments. They are cost-effective and suitable for different waste types. Conversely, laser sensors use light beams to achieve high precision in fill level measurement, offering greater accuracy and range, making them ideal for complex waste profiles. Both types enhance operational efficiency and waste management processes.</p></p>
