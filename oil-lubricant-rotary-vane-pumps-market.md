<p><p>The oil lubricant rotary vane pumps market is segmented into single-stage and two-stage pumps. Single-stage oil lubricant rotary vane pumps typically consist of one working chamber and are designed for low to moderate vacuum applications, providing efficient and reliable performance. In contrast, two-stage oil lubricant rotary vane pumps feature two distinct chambers, allowing for deeper vacuum creation and improved efficiency, making them suitable for more demanding applications. Both types are crucial in various industrial processes for maintaining optimal lubrication and performance.</p></p>
