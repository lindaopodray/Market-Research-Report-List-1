<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic adhesives market comprises two primary types: solvent-based and water-based adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives utilize organic solvents to dissolve the adhesive material, providing strong bonding and quick drying times, making them suitable for industrial applications. Conversely, water-based adhesives use water as a primary solvent, offering a more environmentally friendly option with lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions. They are ideal for applications requiring safety and ease of cleanup, such as packaging and woodworking. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
