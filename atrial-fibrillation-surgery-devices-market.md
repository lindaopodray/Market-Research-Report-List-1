<p><p>The Atrial Fibrillation Surgery Devices market includes Catheter Ablation and Maze Surgery as prominent treatment options. Catheter Ablation involves using energy to destroy small areas of heart tissue that cause irregular rhythms, improving heart function. In contrast, Maze Surgery entails creating scar tissue within the heart to redirect electrical signals, preventing AF episodes. Both methods are designed to restore normal heart rhythm, reduce symptoms, and lower stroke risk, catering to diverse patient needs in managing atrial fibrillation.</p></p>
