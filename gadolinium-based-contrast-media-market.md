<p><p>The Gadolinium-based Contrast Media market is categorized into three main types: Extracellular Agents, Blood Pool Agents, and Hepatobiliary Agents. Extracellular agents are used for various imaging procedures, enhancing the visibility of tissues and organs. Blood Pool Agents provide prolonged circulation time, allowing for detailed vascular imaging. Hepatobiliary Agents target the liver and gallbladder, offering specific insights for hepatobiliary studies. Each type plays a crucial role in diagnostic imaging, improving the accuracy and effectiveness of medical evaluations.</p></p>
