<p><p>The residential smart glass market primarily includes three types: electrochromic glass, thermochromic glass, and photochromic glass. Electrochromic glass changes its tint in response to electrical current, allowing for adjustable privacy and light control. Thermochromic glass reacts to temperature variations, automatically darkening to minimize heat gain. Photochromic glass adjusts its tint based on UV light exposure, enhancing UV protection and glare reduction. Each type offers unique benefits for energy efficiency and comfort in residential settings.</p></p>
