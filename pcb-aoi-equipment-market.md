<p><strong>The PCB AOI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline</li><li>Offline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PCB Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment is categorized into inline and offline markets. Inline AOI systems are integrated directly into the PCB production line, facilitating real-time inspection and immediate feedback during manufacturing. This enhances efficiency and minimizes defects. Offline AOI, on the other hand, involves inspecting PCBs after production in separate, dedicated workstations. While it allows for thorough analysis and additional testing, it can slow down overall production speed compared to inline systems. Both types serve crucial roles in quality control.</p></p>
