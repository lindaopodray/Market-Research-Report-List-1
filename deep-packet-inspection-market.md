<p><p>Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) is categorized into two main market types: Standalone DPI and Integrated DPI. Standalone DPI refers to dedicated systems that focus solely on analyzing data packets for insights on traffic management, security, and compliance. In contrast, Integrated DPI combines DPI capabilities with existing network infrastructure, such as routers and switches, enhancing their functionality without requiring additional hardware. This integration offers a more seamless approach to network management while also optimizing network performance and security measures.</p></p>
