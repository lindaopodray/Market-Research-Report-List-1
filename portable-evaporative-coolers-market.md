<p><ul><li>Combined Flow</li><li>Parallel Flow</li><li>Counter Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable evaporative coolers can be categorized into three types based on airflow configurations: combined flow, parallel flow, and counter flow. Combined flow coolers mix incoming and outgoing air for enhanced cooling, making them versatile for varied environments. Parallel flow coolers maintain separate air streams for cooling and exhaust, optimizing efficiency in larger spaces. Counter flow systems ensure that incoming air flows opposite the exhaust airflow, maximizing evaporative cooling effectiveness. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to diverse cooling needs.</p></p>
