<p><p>The commercial vinyl flooring market includes two primary types: heterogeneous and homogeneous sheet flooring. Heterogeneous sheet flooring consists of multiple layers with varied designs and textures, offering enhanced aesthetics and comfort, often used in spaces requiring durability and style. In contrast, homogeneous sheet flooring features a single-layer construction, providing uniform color and pattern throughout, making it highly resilient and easy to maintain. Both types cater to diverse commercial applications, balancing functionality and design to meet specific needs.</p></p>
