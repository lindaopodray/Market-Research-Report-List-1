<p><p>The laptop cooling pads market features two main types: active cooling pads and passive cooling pads. Active cooling pads incorporate built-in fans that enhance airflow, effectively dissipating heat generated during intensive tasks, thereby improving performance and extending laptop lifespan. In contrast, passive cooling pads rely on materials that absorb and dissipate heat naturally without mechanical parts. While passive options are typically quieter and more energy-efficient, active cooling pads are favored for high-performance laptops that require additional cooling capabilities.</p></p>
