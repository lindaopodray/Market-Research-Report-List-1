<p><p>Sampling systems are categorized into three main market types: liquid, gas, and powder sampling systems. Liquid sampling systems are designed for the collection of fluids in various industries, ensuring accurate and representative samples. Gas sampling systems focus on the capture and analysis of gaseous substances, critical for environmental monitoring and quality control. Powder sampling systems facilitate the collection of solid particulate materials, commonly used in pharmaceuticals and food production. Each type has specialized applications tailored to the specific characteristics of the sampled medium.</p></p>
