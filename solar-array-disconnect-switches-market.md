<p><strong>The Solar Array Disconnect Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Disconnect Switch</li><li>DC Disconnect Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar array disconnect switch market comprises two primary types: AC disconnect switches and DC disconnect switches. AC disconnect switches are used to safely isolate the alternating current generated by solar panels from the inverter and electrical grid, ensuring safe maintenance and operation. In contrast, DC disconnect switches serve to disconnect the direct current from the solar panels before it reaches the inverter, offering protection against electrical faults and facilitating safe system maintenance. Both types are essential for solar energy systems' safety and efficiency.</p></p>
