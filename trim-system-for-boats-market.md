<p><strong>The Trim System for Boats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trim Tabs</li><li>Interceptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The trim system for boats primarily includes two types: trim tabs and interceptors. Trim tabs are adjustable surfaces mounted on the transom that help control the boat's angle in the water, enhancing stability and fuel efficiency. Interceptors, on the other hand, are more advanced devices that provide quicker response times and better performance by adjusting the boat's attitude without affecting its speed significantly. Both systems aim to improve handling, reduce drag, and enhance overall boating experience.</p></p>
