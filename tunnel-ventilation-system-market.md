<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Tunnel Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transverse Tunnel Ventilation</li><li>Semi-Transverse Tunnel Ventilation</li><li>Longitudinal Ventilation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tunnel ventilation systems are essential for maintaining air quality and safety in underground environments. </p><p>Transverse tunnel ventilation involves the use of fans positioned across the tunnel to distribute air evenly, effectively controlling smoke and pollutants. </p><p>Semi-transverse ventilation combines aspects of both transverse and longitudinal systems, providing flexible airflow control. </p><p>Longitudinal ventilation relies on fans positioned along the tunnel's length, creating a unidirectional airflow that helps evacuate smoke and ensure fresh air circulation. Each type is tailored for specific operational needs and safety standards.</p></p>
