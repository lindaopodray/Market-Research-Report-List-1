<p><p>The **Laminate Adhesive Market** is segmented into three main types: **Solvent-Based**, **Solventless**, and **Water-Based** adhesives. **Solvent-Based** adhesives use organic solvents for bonding, offering strong adhesion and durability but raising environmental and health concerns. **Solventless** adhesives are eco-friendly, free from volatile compounds, and provide excellent performance with low emissions. **Water-Based** adhesives use water as a carrier, making them safer and more sustainable, though they may require longer drying times. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing performance, safety, and environmental impact.</p></p>
