<p><strong>The Dental Low speed Handpiece Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Handpiece</li><li>Bend Handpiece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental low-speed handpiece market is classified into two main types: straight handpieces and bend handpieces. Straight handpieces offer a direct approach for various procedures, allowing precise control for polishing and finishing tasks. They are commonly used in restorative dentistry. In contrast, bend handpieces feature an angled design, providing better access to difficult areas in the oral cavity, enhancing maneuverability during procedures like cavity preparation. Both types are essential for different dental applications, optimizing clinician efficiency and patient care.</p></p>
