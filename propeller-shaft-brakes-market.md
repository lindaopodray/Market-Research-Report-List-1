<p><ul><li>Disk Brakes</li><li>Calliper Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The propeller shaft brakes market includes two primary types: disk brakes and caliper brakes. Disk brakes consist of a rotating disc and stationary pads that clamp down on the disc to provide braking force, offering efficient heat dissipation and consistent performance. Caliper brakes, on the other hand, utilize a caliper that exerts pressure on either side of a rotor, ensuring effective braking with a lightweight design. Both types enhance vehicle safety and performance in various automotive applications.</p></p>
