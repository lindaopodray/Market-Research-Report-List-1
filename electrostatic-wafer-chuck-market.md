<p><strong>The Electrostatic Wafer Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrostatic wafer chuck market comprises two main types: Coulomb type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type. Coulomb type chucks utilize electrostatic forces to hold wafers in place, providing efficient and uniform clamping, ideal for various semiconductor processes. In contrast, Johnsen-Rahbek type chucks employ a dielectric layer to enhance adhesion, allowing for stronger and more stable wafer retention. Both types cater to the semiconductor industry, enhancing production efficiency and precision in wafer handling during manufacturing processes.</p></p>
