<p><p>Ice blasting machines are categorized into two primary types: dry ice blasting machines and wet ice blasting machines. Dry ice blasting machines utilize solid carbon dioxide pellets to remove contaminants, offering a non-abrasive cleaning method that sublimates on contact, leaving no residue. In contrast, wet ice blasting machines use a mixture of ice and water, providing a more aggressive cleaning approach suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types are employed across various industries for effective surface cleaning and maintenance.</p></p>
