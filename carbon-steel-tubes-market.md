<p><p>The carbon steel tubes market is primarily categorized into welded and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are manufactured by joining flat steel sheets or plates, making them suitable for various applications due to their cost-effectiveness and versatility. In contrast, seamless tubes are produced from solid steel billets, offering superior strength and uniformity, ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types serve different industries, including construction, automotive, and manufacturing, catering to specific structural and functional requirements based on their unique properties.</p></p>
