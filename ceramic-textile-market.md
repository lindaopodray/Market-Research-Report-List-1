<p><p>The ceramic textile market encompasses various types, including refractory ceramic fibers (RCF), low bio-persistent fibers, and polycrystalline fibers. RCFs are high-temperature resistant materials primarily used for thermal insulation in industrial applications. Low bio-persistent fibers have improved safety profiles, offering reduced biological impact while maintaining thermal performance. Polycrystalline fibers exhibit high thermal stability and resistance to chemical environments, making them suitable for demanding applications. Together, these fiber types cater to diverse industrial needs, prioritizing performance and safety.</p></p>
