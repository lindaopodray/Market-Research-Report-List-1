<p><strong>The PV Water Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Suction</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV water pumps market comprises two primary types: surface suction pumps and submersible pumps. Surface suction pumps are installed above ground and draw water from a source using suction, making them suitable for shallow water applications. In contrast, submersible pumps are submerged in the water source, pushing water to the surface through pressure, which is ideal for deep wells or reservoirs. Both types leverage photovoltaic technology to harness solar energy, promoting sustainable and efficient water pumping solutions.</p></p>
