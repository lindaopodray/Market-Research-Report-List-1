<p><p>Gas detector tubes are analytical devices used for measuring the concentration of gases in various environments. The market is primarily divided into two types: colorimetric gas detector tubes and dosimetric gas detector tubes. Colorimetric tubes change color in response to specific gas concentrations, offering immediate visual results, while dosimetric tubes quantify exposure over time, providing average concentration levels. Both types are essential for safety and compliance in industries such as manufacturing, construction, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
