<p><p>The UV-Vis detector market includes various types such as Photodiode Array (PDA), Refractive Index (RI), and others. PDA detectors measure absorbance across a wide wavelength range, offering high sensitivity and versatility for complex analyses. RI detectors monitor changes in the refractive index of a sample, ideal for non-UV-absorbing compounds. Other detectors include fixed-wavelength and variable-wavelength types, catering to specific analytical needs. Each type serves distinct applications in pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and environmental testing, ensuring precise and reliable detection in diverse analytical workflows.</p></p>
