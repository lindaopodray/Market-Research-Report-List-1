Squeezing slitting employs pressure to separate materials without significant blade contact, reducing wear and ensuring clean cuts, often used for delicate materials. Round knife slitting utilizes circular blades for versatile cutting and is effective for thicker materials, providing smooth edges and high efficiency. Each type addresses unique industry needs, enhancing productivity.</p></p>
