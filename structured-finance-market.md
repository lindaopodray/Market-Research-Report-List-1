<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Structured Finance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Assets Backed Securities (ABS)</li><li>Collateralized Debt Obligations (CDO)</li><li>Mortgage-Backed Securities (MBS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Structured finance encompasses various market types that enhance liquidity and manage risk. Asset-Backed Securities (ABS) pool loans or receivables, such as auto loans or credit card debt, and sell them as securities. Collateralized Debt Obligations (CDO) are complex instruments backed by a diversified portfolio of fixed-income assets, distributing risk among different tranches. Mortgage-Backed Securities (MBS) specifically pool mortgage loans, allowing investors to gain exposure to the real estate market while providing issuers with immediate capital. Each type serves distinct investment and risk management purposes.</p></p>
