<p><p>The metal surgical cannulae market is primarily categorized into two types: curved and straight cannulae. Curved cannulae are designed for procedures requiring navigation around anatomical structures, providing enhanced access and maneuverability. They are often used in minimally invasive surgeries. In contrast, straight cannulae offer direct access to target sites, making them preferable for straightforward procedures. Both types are critical in various surgical disciplines, including plastic surgery, dialysis, and vascular access, enabling precision and efficiency during medical interventions.</p></p>
