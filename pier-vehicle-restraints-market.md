<p><p>The Pier Vehicle Restraints market is categorized into three main types: hydraulic, electric, and mechanical. Hydraulic restraints utilize pressurized fluid to secure vehicles, providing strength and efficiency. Electric restraints function through an electric mechanism, offering enhanced automation and ease of use, often integrated with loading dock systems. Mechanical restraints rely on physical components like chains or bars, providing a reliable, no-power-required option. Each type serves to improve safety and operational efficiency at loading docks by preventing vehicle movement during loading and unloading.</p></p>
