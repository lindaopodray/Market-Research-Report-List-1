<p><p>The Portable Exhaust Gas Analyzer market features various technologies for effective emissions monitoring. The NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) method detects gas concentrations through infrared absorption. The Electrochemical method utilizes chemical reactions to measure gas levels, providing high sensitivity for specific gases. The Hydrogen Flame Ionization method measures hydrocarbons by ionizing gas in a flame, while the Chemiluminescence method quantifies nitrogen oxides through light emission from chemical reactions. Each method offers distinct advantages and applications for environmental and industrial monitoring.</p></p>
