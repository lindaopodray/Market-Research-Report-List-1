<p><strong>The Water Trading Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long-Term Leases</li><li>Short-Term Leases</li><li>Permanent Transfers</li><li>Callable Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water trading encompasses various market types to manage water resources effectively. Long-term leases involve extended agreements where water rights are rented for several years. Short-term leases are temporary arrangements, often for a season or specific needs. Permanent transfers involve the complete sale of water rights, permanently changing ownership. The callable transfer market allows sellers to temporarily transfer water rights with an option to reclaim them later, providing flexibility while facilitating immediate access to water resources.</p></p>
