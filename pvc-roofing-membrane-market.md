<p><p>The PVC roofing membrane market comprises two primary types: glass fiber reinforced PVC membrane and without reinforcement PVC membrane. Glass fiber reinforced PVC provides enhanced durability and strength, making it ideal for high-stress applications and areas prone to extreme weather. In contrast, the without reinforcement PVC membrane is lightweight and flexible, suitable for simpler installations and lower-load roofing scenarios. Both types offer excellent waterproofing, UV resistance, and energy efficiency, catering to various roofing needs in commercial and residential sectors.</p></p>
