<p><p>The chiller market comprises various types, each designed for specific cooling applications. Screw chillers, known for reliability and efficiency, are suitable for medium to large capacities. Scroll chillers offer compact designs and lower capacity cooling, ideal for residential and light commercial use. Centrifugal chillers excel in large-scale industrial applications due to their high efficiency. Absorption chillers utilize heat sources for cooling, making them eco-friendly. Reciprocating chillers are versatile and effective for smaller applications, providing reliable performance across various industries.</p></p>
