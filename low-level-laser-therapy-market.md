<p><strong>The Low Level Laser Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Laser</li><li>Pulse Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Level Laser Therapy (LLLT) market is divided into two primary types: continuous lasers and pulse lasers. Continuous lasers emit a steady beam of light, providing consistent energy delivery, which is beneficial for deeper tissue penetration and prolonged treatment. In contrast, pulse lasers release energy in short bursts, allowing for targeted treatment with reduced heat buildup, making them suitable for sensitive areas. Both types are utilized across various applications such as pain management, wound healing, and cosmetic procedures.</p></p>
