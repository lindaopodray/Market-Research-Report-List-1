<p><p>The organic marigold oleoresin market offers various extraction methods, primarily including solvent extraction and supercritical fluid extraction (SFE). Solvent extraction involves using organic solvents to dissolve and separate the oleoresin from plant material, producing high yields but potentially leaving solvent residues. In contrast, SFE utilizes supercritical CO2, ensuring a purer product without harmful solvents, retaining more volatile compounds and enhancing aroma and flavor. Both methods cater to diverse industries such as cosmetics, food, and pharmaceuticals, reflecting varying quality and purity levels.</p></p>
