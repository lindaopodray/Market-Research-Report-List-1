<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Sports Drinks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic Sport Drink</li><li>Hypertonic Sport Drink</li><li>Hypotonic Sport Drink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sports drinks market comprises three main types: isotonic, hypertonic, and hypotonic drinks. Isotonic drinks contain similar concentrations of sugars and salts as body fluids, making them ideal for quick hydration and energy replenishment during exercise. Hypertonic drinks have higher sugar and electrolyte concentrations, suitable for recovery post-exercise, providing concentrated energy. Hypotonic drinks have lower sugar and electrolyte levels, allowing for rapid hydration with less energy, making them ideal for low-intensity activities. Each type caters to specific hydration and energy needs based on activity levels.</p></p>
