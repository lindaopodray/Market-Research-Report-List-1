<p><p>Polymer-Dispersed Liquid Crystals (PDLCs) are materials where liquid crystals are dispersed in a polymer matrix, enabling unique optical properties. The market includes Scattering PDLCs, which scatter light when not electrically activated, making them ideal for privacy applications. Nano-PDLCs feature nanometer-sized liquid crystal droplets, enhancing light transmission and responsiveness. Polymer Network Liquid Crystals consist of a cross-linked polymer network that stabilizes the liquid crystal orientation, offering improved thermal stability and faster switching times, catering to diverse display and smart window applications.</p></p>
