<p><ul><li>Wet DCT</li><li>Dry DCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive dual-clutch transmission (DCT) market is divided into two main types: wet DCT and dry DCT. Wet DCT systems use a lubricating fluid reservoir to cool and lubricate the clutches, enabling higher power handling and durability, making them suitable for performance vehicles. In contrast, dry DCT systems operate without fluid cooling, resulting in lighter weight and reduced complexity, typically found in smaller, more fuel-efficient cars. Each type caters to different performance and efficiency needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
