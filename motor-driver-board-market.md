<p><p>The motor driver board market includes various types designed for controlling motors in different applications. H-Bridge motor driver boards enable bidirectional control of a single DC motor by allowing current to flow in both directions, making them ideal for robotic systems. Dual H-Bridge motor driver boards manage two separate motors simultaneously, providing enhanced control for more complex applications like robotic arms or automated vehicles. Both types are critical for enabling precise motor control in electronics and automation projects.</p></p>
