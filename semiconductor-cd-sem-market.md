<p><p>The Semiconductor CD-SEM (Critical Dimension Scanning Electron Microscope) market is divided into two main types: Photomask CD-SEM and Wafer CD-SEM. Photomask CD-SEM focuses on inspecting and measuring photomasks used in semiconductor manufacturing to ensure precise patterning. Wafer CD-SEM, on the other hand, is used for evaluating critical dimensions on wafers after the lithography process, ensuring that the features meet the stringent specifications required for high-performance semiconductor devices. Both types play essential roles in quality control and process optimization.</p></p>
