<p><strong>The Automotive Invisible Paint Protection Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVC Type</li><li>PU Type</li><li>TPU Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive invisible paint protection film market features three primary types: PVC, PU, and TPU. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) offers cost-effective protection but may lack flexibility and durability. PU (polyurethane) is more resilient, providing excellent clarity and self-healing properties, making it a popular choice for car enthusiasts. TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) combines the benefits of both, offering high flexibility, durability, and resistance to yellowing. Each type serves different consumer needs, balancing cost, protection level, and longevity in paint preservation.</p></p>
