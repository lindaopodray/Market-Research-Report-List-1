<p><ul><li>Reflective condenser</li><li>Refracting condenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Luminescent Solar Concentrator (LSC) market is divided into two primary types: reflective condensers and refracting condensers. Reflective condensers utilize mirrors or reflective surfaces to direct sunlight onto a luminescent material, enhancing light capture and improving energy conversion efficiency. In contrast, refracting condensers use materials that bend light, guiding sunlight into the luminescent layer. Both types aim to increase solar energy utilization in various applications, contributing to renewable energy growth and efficiency advancements in solar technologies.</p></p>
