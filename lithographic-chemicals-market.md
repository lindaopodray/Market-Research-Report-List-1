<p><p>The lithographic chemicals market encompasses various types essential for semiconductor manufacturing. Removers are used to eliminate unwanted materials during processing. Deposition precursors facilitate the deposition of thin films on substrates. Etchants selectively remove layers to create microstructures, while dopants introduce impurities to modify electrical properties. Photoresists (ESIs) are sensitive materials applied to capture patterns during exposure. Other chemicals may include developers and surface modifiers, all contributing to precise fabrication and enhancing the performance of integrated circuits.</p></p>
