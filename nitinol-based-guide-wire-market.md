<p><p>The Nitinol guide wire market is categorized into three main types: angled, J-shaped, and straight guide wires. Angled guide wires feature a curved tip that aids in navigating complex anatomical pathways, making them suitable for specific interventional procedures. J-shaped guide wires offer a predefined curve, enhancing maneuverability around bends in vascular systems. Straight guide wires, with their linear design, provide direct access and support for various diagnostic and therapeutic interventions, ensuring versatility in medical applications across different specialties.</p></p>
