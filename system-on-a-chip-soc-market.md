<p><p>A System on a Chip (SoC) integrates all components of a computer or electronic system onto a single chip. The market can be divided into digital and analog systems. Digital SoCs focus on processing, memory, and digital communication, predominantly used in smartphones and computers. In contrast, analog SoCs handle real-world signals, including audio, video, and sensor data, commonly found in devices like amplifiers and radio-frequency systems. Both types cater to diverse applications, driving innovation in electronics and consumer technologies.</p></p>
