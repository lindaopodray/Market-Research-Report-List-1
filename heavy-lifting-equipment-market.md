<p><p>The heavy lifting equipment market is segmented into three main types: manually operated, hydraulic, and electrical equipment. Manually operated tools rely on human strength for lifting and moving heavy loads, often found in smaller-scale applications. Hydraulic equipment utilizes fluid pressure to lift substantial weights, providing efficiency and power, commonly used in construction and industrial settings. Electrical equipment employs motors and machinery to automate lifting processes, enhancing productivity and safety, particularly in larger operations where heavy loads are frequent.</p></p>
