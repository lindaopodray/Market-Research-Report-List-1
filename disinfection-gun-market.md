<p><p>The disinfection gun market is categorized into two main types: wet mist and dry mist. Wet mist disinfection guns release a fine spray of liquid disinfectant, effectively covering surfaces and reaching crevices for thorough sanitation. In contrast, dry mist disinfection guns generate a vaporized aerosol solution, allowing for quicker drying times and minimal residue. Both types are utilized in various settings, including hospitals, schools, and public spaces, to enhance hygiene and reduce disease transmission effectively.</p></p>
