<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Bypass Relay market includes two primary types: Single Mode and Multimode. Single Mode fibers utilize a single light path, enabling longer transmission distances and higher bandwidth, making them ideal for long-range telecommunications. In contrast, Multimode fibers support multiple light paths, which is beneficial for shorter distances and lower costs, typically used in local area networks. These distinct types cater to varying communication needs, with Single Mode focusing on long-haul applications and Multimode suited for short-range data transfers.</p></p>
