<p><ul><li>Adaptive Front-lighting Headlight</li><li>Adaptive Driving Beam Headlight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive smart headlights market includes advanced lighting technologies such as Adaptive Front-lighting Systems (AFS) and Adaptive Driving Beam (ADB) headlights. AFS adjusts the light direction and intensity based on vehicle speed, steering angle, and elevation, enhancing visibility during curves and inclines. In contrast, ADB systems utilize camera sensors to autonomously control individual LED beams, optimizing illumination while preventing glare for oncoming drivers. Both technologies aim to improve safety, enhance driving comfort, and adapt to varying road conditions.</p></p>
