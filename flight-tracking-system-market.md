<p><p>The Flight Tracking System market includes technologies like ADS-B, FANS, and PFTS. ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast) uses satellite signals to provide real-time aircraft position data, enhancing air traffic control efficiency. FANS (Future Air Navigation System) integrates satellite communication and navigation for oceanic and remote airspace, enabling data link communication. PFTS (Portable Flight Tracking Systems) are compact, independent devices used for tracking aircraft in areas with limited infrastructure. Each system caters to specific operational needs, improving safety, efficiency, and global airspace management.</p></p>
