<p><p>The skin resurfacing machine market is primarily categorized into two types: ablative and non-ablative. Ablative machines remove the outer layers of skin to promote healing and collagen production, commonly used for severe skin issues such as deep wrinkles and scars. Non-ablative machines, on the other hand, target the underlying skin without damaging the surface, making them suitable for mild issues like superficial wrinkles and skin tone irregularities. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to different skin concerns and patient preferences.</p></p>
