<p><ul><li>Spay Through Overcaps</li><li>Traditional "Dust-Cover" Overcaps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerosol overcap market consists of two main types: spray-through overcaps and traditional "dust-cover" overcaps. Spray-through overcaps allow for direct application of the aerosol product without removing the cap, enhancing convenience and efficiency. In contrast, traditional dust-cover overcaps primarily serve to protect the aerosol valve from contamination, dust, and damage during storage and transportation. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the proper functioning and longevity of aerosol products while catering to different user preferences and application needs.</p></p>
