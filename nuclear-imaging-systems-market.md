<p><strong>The Nuclear Imaging Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positron Emission Tomography</li><li>Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography</li><li>Planar Scintigraphy Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nuclear imaging systems encompass various technologies used for diagnosing and monitoring medical conditions through imaging. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) utilizes radioactive tracers to visualize metabolic activity in tissues, crucial for cancer detection and management. Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) provides 3D imaging by detecting gamma rays, often used in cardiac studies and brain imaging. Planar Scintigraphy offers 2D images to assess organ function and abnormalities, commonly used in thyroid and bone imaging, providing vital information for patient care.</p></p>
