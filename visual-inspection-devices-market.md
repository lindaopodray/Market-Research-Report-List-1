<p><p>The Visual Inspection Devices market comprises two main types: Nondestructive Testing (NDT) and Remote Visual Monitoring (RVM). NDT employs various techniques to analyze materials and structures without causing damage, ensuring integrity and safety in sectors such as manufacturing and construction. RVM, on the other hand, utilizes cameras and sensors to perform inspections from a distance, allowing for the examination of hard-to-reach or hazardous areas, increasing efficiency and safety while minimizing downtime during maintenance or inspection processes.</p></p>
