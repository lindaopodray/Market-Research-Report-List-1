<p><strong>The Mechanical Absolute Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Singleturn Encoder</li><li>Multiturn Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical absolute encoders market is divided into two main types: multiturn encoders and singleturn encoders. Singleturn encoders provide a unique position value for each rotation within a single turn, typically measuring 360 degrees. In contrast, multiturn encoders track position over multiple turns, incorporating additional mechanisms to retain position even when power is lost, often through gear systems. Both types serve critical roles in automation, robotics, and industrial applications, ensuring accurate position feedback and control.</p></p>
