<p><p>The roof anchor point market is divided into two primary types: permanent and temporary roof anchor points. Permanent roof anchor points are fixed installations designed for long-term use, providing reliable safety for workers accessing rooftops for maintenance or construction. These anchors are typically integrated into the building's structure. In contrast, temporary roof anchor points are portable and adjustable, meant for short-term applications and quick setups.
