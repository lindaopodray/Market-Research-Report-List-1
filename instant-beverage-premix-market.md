<p><p>The Instant Beverage Premix market comprises three primary types: powder, paste, and granules. Powder premixes are fine, easily dissolvable products often used for beverages like coffee or tea, offering convenience and long shelf life. Paste premixes, thicker in consistency, are typically used for richer flavored drinks, providing a more robust taste. Granules, coarser than powder, maintain flavor potency and are used for instant beverages like soups or sauces that require minimal preparation effort. Each type caters to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
