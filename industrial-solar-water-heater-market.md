<p><strong>The Industrial Solar Water Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active System</li><li>Passive System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial solar water heater market comprises two main types: active and passive systems. Active systems utilize pumps and controllers to circulate water and maximize efficiency, making them suitable for large-scale applications. They often feature advanced technologies for better temperature control. In contrast, passive systems rely on natural thermosiphon principles, where hot water rises without mechanical assistance. These systems are simpler, more cost-effective, and require less maintenance, making them ideal for smaller operations or locations with less demanding heating needs.</p></p>
